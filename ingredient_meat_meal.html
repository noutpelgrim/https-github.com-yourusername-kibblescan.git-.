Meat Meal (Generic)

NON-SPECIFIC DECLARATION Class: Rendered Protein Source Verification Failed

Standard Definition

The rendered product from mammal tissues, exclusive of any added blood, hair, hoof, horn, hide trimmings, manure, stomach and rumen contents.

Key Ambiguity: The definition does not specify the animal species (e.g., Cow, Pig, Sheep, Goat). It may be any combination thereof.

Scientific Uncertainty Profile

This ingredient is not classified as "Toxic," but as "Obscured." The lack of species identification introduces two specific risks:

  • Allergen Masking: An owner attempting to avoid Beef or Pork cannot confirm absence in this ingredient.
  • Nutritional Variability: The amino acid profile varies significantly depending on the ratio of bone-to-muscle in the rendered batch.

Surveillance Vectors

CAS: 1406-18-4 | NATURAL ANTIOXIDANT

Vitamin E (Mixed Tocopherols)

UNRESTRICTED Safe / Essential

Clinical Definition

A complex of alpha, beta, gamma, and delta tocopherols derived from plant oils. Used primarily as a natural preservative to prevent fat oxidation in kibble. It is biologically active and safe, unlike synthetic preservatives (BHA/BHT).

Function

  • Preservation: Inhibits lipid peroxidation, keeping fats fresh.
  • Immune Support: Acts as a scavenger of free radicals in the body.
  • Bioavailability: High retention rate compared to synthetic acetate forms.

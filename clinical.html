01. Why It Exists

Clinical diet recommendations are often undermined by silent formulation changes in over-the-counter products. Manufacturers are not legally required to notify practitioners of ingredient drift. This platform fills that informational gap.

02. How It Works

We utilize optical surveillance to track 12,000+ formulations. When a client scans a product, it is cross-referenced against expected values. Deviations (Drift) are flagged immediately for clinical review.

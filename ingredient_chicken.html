TAXONOMY: Gallus gallus domesticus | AAFCO ID: 9.57

Chicken (Whole / Mechanically Separated)

UNRESTRICTED Class: Animal Protein Active Monitoring

Standard Definition

The clean combination of flesh and skin with or without accompanying bone, derived from the parts or whole carcasses of poultry or a combination thereof. It is exclusive of feathers, heads, feet, and entrails.

Toxicological Status

Current Limits:

No adverse effect levels (NOAEL) established for this ingredient in canine/feline models. It is considered a foundational biological substrate.

Active Surveillance Vectors

While the ingredient definition is unrestricted, KibbleScan monitors this node for specific supply chain deviations:

  • Pentobarbital Contamination: Historical instances of euthanasia drugs entering the rendering stream.
  • Salmonella / Listeria: Bacterial load exceeding raw consumption thresholds.
  • Sourcing Drift: Replacement of "Chicken" with "Chicken By-Product Meal" (a lower-cost derivative).

ID: GRAIN-05 | COMPLEX CARBOHYDRATE

Whole Grain Brown Rice

UNRESTRICTED Standard Nutrient

Clinical Definition

The entire grain of rice including the bran, germ, and endosperm. A source of fiber, manganese, and selenium. Generally well-tolerated and digestible for most canines, unlike corn or wheat gluten.

Nutritional Profile

  • Glycemic Index: Moderate (Lower than white rice).
  • Fiber: Contains insoluble fiber aiding peristalsis.
  • Arsenic Monitor: Routine batch testing required for heavy metal compliance.

Registry Status

Prevalence in Index 31.5% of Formulas
